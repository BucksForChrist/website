Mission

We are a campus ministry serving UA Rich Mountain. We believe in sharing the Good News of Jesus Christ with college students.This is a place for students to connect with, same age, like-minded people to share in the greatest story ever written, the good news of Jesus Christ. Our goal is to love people, share God, and grow disciples. Our prayer is that God's will be done to influence the next generation to seek the truth, and spread the kingdom of God.

Bucks For Christ is a ministry of the Northside Church of Christ
